# The Great Tomato Adventure

Gabu and Daru are two young tortoises. They are brothers, but they are very different.

Gabu is big and fast. Well, he is fast for a tortoise! He loves to explore. Daru is small and quiet. He loves to sleep in the warm sun. They live in a beautiful green garden.

## A Hungry Morning

One sunny morning, Gabu wakes up early. He looks around the garden.
"Daru! Wake up!" Gabu says. "I am hungry. I want to find something good to eat today."

Daru opens his eyes slowly. "Mmm... good morning, Gabu. What do you want to eat?"

"Tomatoes!" Gabu says. "The big, red, sweet tomatoes near the big tree."

## The Long Walk

The tomatoes are far away. For a tortoise, it is a very long walk.

Gabu walks quickly. Step, step, step.
"Come on, Daru! Walk faster!" Gabu calls.

Daru walks slowly. Step... step... step.
"Wait for me, Gabu," Daru says. "My legs are short, and my shell is heavy."

Suddenly, Gabu stops. There is a big brown rock in the grass. Gabu cannot climb over it. He tries, but he falls on his side.
"Oh no! Help me, Daru!" Gabu cries.

## Daru the Hero

Daru walks to his brother. He uses his strong head and pushes Gabu.
One, two, three... push!

Gabu is back on his feet. "Thank you, Daru!" Gabu says. "You are small, but you are very strong."

Daru smiles. "We are a team. Now, let's walk together."

## The Sweet Reward

They walk together slowly. Finally, they arrive at the big tree. Under the green leaves, they see five big, red tomatoes.

Gabu and Daru eat the tomatoes. Chomp, chomp, chomp. The tomatoes are sweet and delicious.

"This is a great day," Daru says, closing his eyes.
"Yes," Gabu says, sitting next to his brother. "You are the best brother in the world."

Now, the two tortoises are full and happy. They sleep together under the warm sun.
