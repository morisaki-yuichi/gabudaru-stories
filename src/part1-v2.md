# The Great Tomato Adventure

Gabu and Daru are two young tortoises. They are friends, but they are very different.

Gabu is big. He always looks carefully before every step. Daru is small, but his legs are quick. He loves to discover new things in the garden. They live in a beautiful green garden.

## A Hungry Morning

One sunny morning, Daru wakes up early. His nose twitches. Something smells wonderful!

"Gabu! Wake up!" Daru says. "I can smell something delicious. Let's go find it!"

Gabu opens his eyes slowly. He looks around carefully. "Mmm... good morning, Daru. What do you smell?"

"Tomatoes!" Daru says. "The big, red, sweet tomatoes near the big tree. Let's go now!"

## The Long Walk

The tomatoes are far away. For a tortoise, it is a very long walk.

Daru walks quickly. Step, step, step.
"Come on, Gabu! This way!" Daru calls.

Gabu walks slowly. Step... step... step.
"Wait, Daru," Gabu says. "We should be careful. Let me check the path first."

Suddenly, there is a loud crash. Daru has run too fast and tipped over onto his side near a big brown rock.
"Oh no! Help me, Gabu!" Daru cries.

## Gabu to the Rescue

Gabu walks slowly and carefully to his friend. He uses his big, strong body and pushes Daru.
One, two, three... push!

Daru is back on his feet. "Thank you, Gabu!" Daru says. "You are big and strong. You always know what to do."

Gabu smiles. "We are a team. Now, let's walk together."

## The Sweet Reward

They walk together. Daru walks a little slower. Gabu follows carefully beside him. Finally, they arrive at the big tree. Under the green leaves, they see five big, red tomatoes.

Gabu and Daru eat the tomatoes. Chomp, chomp, chomp. The tomatoes are sweet and delicious.

"This is a great day," Daru says, closing his eyes.
"Yes," Gabu says, sitting next to his friend. "You are the best friend in the world."

Now, the two tortoises are full and happy. They sleep together under the warm sun.
