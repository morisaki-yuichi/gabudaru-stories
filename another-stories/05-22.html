May 22

Found!

🐢 Gabu🐢 Daru

After two mornings of looking for Daru, Gabu decides to have a conversation.

He says: "Daru. You move around at night. I wake up and you are gone."

Daru says: "Yes. I find cool spots."

Gabu says: "Can you tell me where you will sleep? So I know where to look?"

Daru thinks.

He says: "I don't always know before I sleep. I wake up and I am uncomfortable and I move."

Gabu thinks about this.

He says: "Then in the morning, when you wake up, you could come to me. To let me know where you are."

Daru thinks about this.

He says: "That is easy. I can do that."

They agree on this.

The next morning, Daru wakes up under the apple tree. He walks to Gabu.

"Good morning. I am under the apple tree."

"Good morning," says Gabu. "Thank you."

They eat breakfast together.

Gabu thinks: it is not about knowing every moment where Daru is. It is about having a way to find each other easily.

He thinks: in a family, you need ways to find each other.

He says this to Daru.

Daru says: "Are we a family?"

Gabu is quiet for a moment.

He says: "I think so. A small one. But yes."

Daru is quiet too. Then he smiles.

"A family," says Daru. "Yes."

They walk together to the pond.

Gabu closes his eyes.

He is warm inside.

He sleeps.
