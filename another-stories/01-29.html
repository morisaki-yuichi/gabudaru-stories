January 29

A Strange Sound

🐢 Gabu

Gabu is sitting by the pond when he hears a sound.

It is coming from outside the wall. He has never heard this sound before. He is not sure what it is.

He stands up and listens very carefully.

The sound is high and thin. Like a voice, but not a bird voice. Not a frog voice. Something else.

He walks to the wall. He presses his ear against the cold stone. He listens.

The sound comes again. Clear and close. Still outside.

He is not afraid. He is curious.

He walks to the gate. He looks through the crack. He can see the path outside. He cannot see anything making the sound.

He waits.

The sound comes again. This time a little louder. And then he hears something else. Small footsteps. Something walking on the path outside the gate.

He pulls back from the gate. He waits.

The footsteps stop. Then he hears the high thin sound one more time. Very close.

He holds very still.

Then silence.

He waits a long time. The sound does not come again. The footsteps do not come again.

He walks away from the gate slowly. He goes back to his flat stone.

He thinks about the sound. What was it? Who was it?

He does not know.

But he felt something when he heard it. A little pull, like the beginning of warmth.

He thinks about it all evening.

"Something was there," he says.

He closes his eyes.

He listens to the quiet garden.

He wonders.

He sleeps.
