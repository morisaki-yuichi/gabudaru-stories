March 26

Gabu Finds a Feather

🐢 Gabu
0:00

Today Gabu finds a feather.

He finds it near the big tree. It is lying on the grass. Long and grey-blue. Soft at the bottom, stiff at the top. A beautiful feather.

He looks at it for a moment. Then he nudges it with his nose. It moves. Very light. He nudges it again.

He picks it up. Well, he cannot really pick things up. He gets it to rest on his nose. He walks slowly with the feather on his nose.

It falls off. He tries again.

He gets it to rest on his shell. More stable there. He walks slowly and carefully with the feather on his shell.

He walks to his flat stone.

He puts the feather down next to his three stones.

Now he has: the big flat stone. The small grey stone. The blue stone. And a feather.

He looks at his collection.

He thinks: the feather is different from the stones. It is not heavy. It is not hard. It will not last forever in the weather.

He thinks: some things are soft and light and they do not last. But they are still beautiful.

He looks at the feather. The grey-blue catches the light.

He says: "You are beautiful."

He knows it will not be here long. The wind will take it, or the rain.

But today it is here. Today it is part of his collection.

He closes his eyes.

He is glad he found it.

He sleeps.
