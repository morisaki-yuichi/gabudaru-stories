December 18

Last Preparations

🐢 Gabu🐢 Daru🐕 Dango🐱 Suke-san

Three days before the solstice.

Gabu and Suke-san went around the house making the last winter preparations.

They checked the woodpile. Full. More than enough.

They checked the food. Root vegetables stored. Dried herbs. The last jar of jam. Dried fruit Gabu had put up in autumn.

They checked the sleeping spots. Warm, insulated.

They checked the windows and door. All sealed.

Dango followed them.

"Is this what you do before the solstice?" it said.

"Before the darkest time," Gabu said. "To make sure everything is ready."

Dango thought about this. "The darkest time is close," it said.

"Three days," Suke-san said.

"And then the light comes back," Dango said.

"Yes."

Dango helped check the woodpile by counting the logs. There were many.

"Enough warmth," it said.

"Yes," said Gabu.

Dango helped check the food. It lined up the jars and bags.

"Enough food," it said.

"Yes," said Gabu.

They walked back through the house.

"Enough of everything," Dango said.

Gabu stopped. He looked at Dango. "Yes," he said. "Enough of everything."

Dango understood that the "everything" was not only food and wood.

Four friends. Enough.

One garden. Enough.

The darkest time was coming. And they were ready.
