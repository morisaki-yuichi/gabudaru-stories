February 22

A Brown Cat

🐢 Gabu

Something is looking at Gabu from the top of the wall.

Gabu is walking near the wall when he sees it. Two bright eyes. Looking down at him.

It is a cat. A brown and orange cat, sitting on top of the wall. Its tail hangs down on one side.

Gabu stops. He looks at the cat.

The cat looks at him.

Neither of them moves for a long time.

The cat is big. Much bigger than Gabu. Its ears are straight up. Its eyes are round and bright.

Gabu is not afraid. He has seen cats on the wall before, sometimes. They usually do not stay long.

"Hello," says Gabu.

The cat says: "Mrrr."

"I am Gabu," says Gabu. "I live here."

The cat looks at him. Then it looks away. Then it looks at him again.

Gabu says: "You are welcome to sit on the wall."

The cat does not seem to need his permission. But Gabu says it anyway.

They stay like this for a while. Gabu on the ground, cat on the wall.

Gabu thinks: the cat can see over the wall. The cat can see my garden and the world outside. It can go in both places.

He says: "What is it like out there?"

The cat says: "Mrrr." Then it yawns.

"I see," says Gabu.

He walks to his flat stone. He can still feel the cat watching him.

He looks back. The cat is still there. Looking at him.

Then the cat stands up, stretches, and steps off the wall to the outside.

Gone.

Gabu sits on his flat stone.

He thinks about the cat.

He closes his eyes.

He sleeps.
