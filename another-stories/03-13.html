March 13

The Tall Grass

🐢 Gabu

The grass is growing now. Really growing.

In winter, the grass was short and yellow. In February, it started to come back. Now in March, it is tall and green.

Gabu walks through the long grass near the north wall. The grass comes up higher than his shell. He moves through it and the grass touches his sides.

He likes this feeling.

He stops in the middle of the long grass. He is surrounded by green. He cannot see much of the garden. Just green grass all around him and blue sky above.

He is in his own small world inside the grass.

He sits very still.

A grasshopper jumps near him. He watches it. It lands on a grass stem. It cleans its back legs. Then it jumps away.

A small blue butterfly flies through the grass. It goes past him and is gone.

A beetle walks along a grass stem, going up.

He thinks: this grass is full of life. Creatures live here that I never see. They live in the grass world.

He stays there for a long time.

Then he walks out of the grass. He comes back to the shorter grass near the path.

He looks back at the long grass. It moves in the wind.

He thinks: the garden has many worlds. The far corner. The grass world. The pond world. The tree world.

He is happy to know them all.

He walks to his flat stone.

He closes his eyes.

He smiles.

He sleeps.
