January 6

First Snow

🐢 Gabu

Gabu wakes up. Something is different. He does not know what. He opens his eyes.

White. Everything is white.

Snow has come in the night. The garden is covered. The ground is white. The stone wall is white. The branches of the big tree have white lines on top.

Gabu puts his foot out. His foot touches something soft and cold.

"Oh," says Gabu.

He has seen snow before. But every time it comes, it surprises him a little.

He walks slowly into the snow. His feet make small round marks. He looks behind him. A line of round prints goes from his stone to where he stands now. He is making a pattern in the white.

He walks a little more. More prints. He turns around. He makes a circle. He looks at the circle of prints. It looks nice.

The snow is cold. His feet are cold. But the rest of him is warm inside his shell.

He walks to the big tree. The snow under the tree is not as deep. The branches kept some of it away.

He looks up. Snow is on every branch. When the wind blows, some falls down. A little falls on Gabu's head. Cold.

"Ah," says Gabu.

He stands very still for a long time. The garden is white and silent. He can hear nothing. Not even the wind.

He thinks: this is very beautiful.

He walks back to his flat stone. The snow has covered it too. He brushes some snow off with his foot. Then he sits on it.

Cold. But his stone. He closes his eyes.

The garden is white and peaceful. Gabu is happy.
