February 15

Gabu's Favorite Food

🐢 Gabu

Gabu is thinking about food today.

He is sitting by the pond in the thin February sun. He is thinking about his favorite food.

What is his favorite food?

He thinks carefully. He likes many things.

He likes sweet berries from the vine on the east wall. The ones in late summer, when they are fully ripe. Deep purple and soft. Sweet and a little bit tart.

He likes fresh green grass in spring. The first new grass of the year. It is tender and bright and tastes of the earth.

He likes mushrooms after rain. The small brown ones that grow near the base of the big tree. Earthy and rich.

He likes the water plants at the edge of the pond. In summer, when they are long and green. Mild and clean.

He is thinking about all these foods and feeling a little hungry.

He eats some moss from the wall. It is not his favorite. But it fills his stomach.

He thinks: in two months or three, the first berries will come. He will wait.

He thinks: the grass is already starting to come. He goes and eats some new grass near the pond.

It is good. Not as good as summer grass. But better than moss.

He thinks: things are better when you wait for them. The summer berries taste better because he has been waiting all winter.

He walks back to his flat stone.

He thinks about his favorite food.

He looks forward to eating it again.

He closes his eyes.

He dreams about ripe purple berries.

He sleeps.
