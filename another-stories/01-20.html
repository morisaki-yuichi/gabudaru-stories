January 20

Gabu's Dream

🐢 Gabu
0:00

Last night Gabu had a dream.

In the dream, the garden was full of flowers. Red and yellow and purple. The grass was long and green. The pond was warm and sparkling.

And in the dream, someone else was in the garden.

Gabu does not know who it was. Just a shape. A small shape, moving through the flowers. He tried to see it clearly. But in dreams, things are not always clear.

The shape moved toward him. He was not afraid. He was happy.

Then he woke up.

He opens his eyes. The garden is grey and cold. No flowers. Short yellow grass. Cold pond.

But Gabu still feels the warmth from the dream.

He thinks about the dream all morning. Who was that shape? A bird? A frog? Someone else?

He does not know.

He walks around the garden. He looks in all the places. The leaf pile. The pond edge. The base of the tree. Under the flat stone.

No one.

Just Gabu. Just his garden.

He sits on his flat stone. He looks at the grey sky.

He thinks: maybe one day someone will come. Someone will be in my garden with me.

He does not know if this will happen. But the dream felt very real.

"That would be nice," says Gabu.

He waits. The sky is quiet. The garden is quiet.

He thinks about the shape in the dream. He tries to remember more. He cannot.

But the warm feeling stays.

He closes his eyes.

He hopes for another dream tonight.

He sleeps, and waits, and hopes.
