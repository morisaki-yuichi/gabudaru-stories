June 1

The First Day of Summer

🐢 Gabu🐢 Daru

It was the first day of June. The air was warm and soft.

Gabu sat in the garden and looked up. The sky was a deep, bright blue. No clouds. Just blue.

"It feels different today," Gabu said.

Daru walked out from under the big leaf. He looked up too. "It is warmer," he said. "A lot warmer."

The sun was already high. It shone on the pond and made little sparks of light on the water.

Gabu went to the pond and put one foot in. The water was cool. It felt very good.

"Come in," Gabu said. "The water is nice."

Daru looked at the pond. He was not sure. But he put one foot in. Then another. Then he sat down in the shallow end.

"Oh," Daru said. "This is very good."

They sat in the pond for a long time. The sun moved slowly across the sky. The sparks of light moved with it.

When they came out, they sat on the warm flat stone near the water. The stone had been in the sun all morning. It was perfectly warm.

"I like June," Daru said.

Gabu smiled. "I thought you would," he said.

The garden smelled like warm grass and flowers. Somewhere far away, a bee was buzzing.

It was a good first day of summer.
