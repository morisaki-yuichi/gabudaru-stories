October 6

Three Together on a Cold Morning

🐢 Gabu🐢 Daru🐕 Dango

October mornings were cold enough now that they did not want to stay outside long.

But every morning, they went out. Even for a few minutes.

They stood together in the garden and breathed the cold air.

Gabu in the middle. Daru on his left. Dango on his right.

Three small warm shapes in the cold October garden.

Dango said: "What do you think about in the morning?"

"What comes next in the day," Daru said. "What I will do."

"I think about the garden," Gabu said. "What it needs. What I notice."

Dango thought about what it thought about.

"I think about everyone I am glad to know," it said.

Gabu and Daru looked at Dango.

"That is what you think about in the morning?" Daru said.

"Yes," Dango said. "It starts the day well."

They stood in the cold.

Gabu thought: I have known this garden for many years. I have stood in it in every October. But I have not stood in it with two others before.

He thought about that.

"I think about everyone I am glad to know too," he said. "This morning."

Dango looked up at him.

"Me too," said Daru.

They stood a little longer in the cold, until their noses were very cold and it was time for breakfast.

Then they went inside to the warm house.
