November 26

A Good November Day

🐢 Gabu🐢 Daru🐕 Dango🐱 Suke-san

Not all November days were grey.

One morning, the sky was clear and blue. The sun came through and lit everything.

The garden sparkled. The frost on the bare branches caught the light and made a thousand small bright points. The pond ice reflected the blue sky.

Dango came outside and stood with its arms out.

"It is beautiful!" it said.

The others came out.

It was cold. But the sun was real, and the light was clear, and the garden in November sun looked like something out of a story.

Suke-san's face opened into something Dango had not seen before — a full, unguarded smile.

"What?" Dango said.

"I was thinking of a morning like this," Suke-san said. "Long ago. When I was walking. I came to the top of a hill and the whole valley was like this."

"Like now?" Dango said.

"Yes. Clear and bright and cold. And I thought then — if I could find a place like this valley, I would stay."

Dango looked at Suke-san. "And then you found here."

"Yes," Suke-san said. "Eventually."

They stood in the November sun until their noses were cold and the clouds came back over.

Then they went inside.

But the image stayed — four turtles in a sparkling winter garden, arms out, in the sun.

Worth waiting the whole grey month for.
