January 1

The First Morning

🐢 Gabu

It is the first day of January. The garden is quiet. The sky is pale and grey. It is cold, but it is not snowing.

Gabu is a small turtle. He lives in a garden with a big tree, a pond, and a long stone wall. Today the flowers are all sleeping. But Gabu is awake.

He opens his eyes slowly. He stretches his four legs. He looks around.

"Good morning, garden," he says.

He walks to his favorite stone. It is a large, flat stone near the big tree. He stands on it and feels the cold under his feet. A small brown bird sits in the tree above him. It looks at Gabu.

"Good morning," says Gabu.

The bird does not say anything. But it stays in the tree. Gabu is happy about that.

He walks to the pond. The water is still and dark. He can see his face in the water. He is a small turtle with a round green shell.

In the afternoon, the sun comes out. It is not very strong. But it is warm on his shell. Gabu closes his eyes and stands very still.

He thinks: this is my garden. This is my home. It is good to be here.

When it gets dark, the bird sings one small song. Then it flies away.

Gabu walks back to his stone. He is a little tired. He had a good day.

He closes his eyes. The garden is quiet and peaceful. Gabu sleeps.
