March 30

Getting Ready

🐢 Gabu

Gabu has a feeling today. The feeling has been building for days. Now it is strong.

Something is going to change. Something new is coming. He can feel it.

He does not know what.

He walks around his garden and thinks about it. Is he ready?

He looks at his flat stone. His three stones. His feather. Ready.

He looks at the flowers on the south wall. Many flowers now. Ready.

He looks at the pond. The frogs are active. The water is warm and green. Ready.

He looks at the big tree. Full of fresh green leaves. Ready.

He looks at the apple tree. Still some flowers. Petals falling. Behind them, tiny green apple buds starting to form.

He looks at the gate.

He walks to the gate. He looks at the gap at the bottom. Big enough for a mouse. Big enough for small things.

He says: "I am ready."

He does not know what he is ready for. He just knows he is.

He walks to the middle of the garden.

He stands there.

The spring garden is all around him. Green and warm and alive. The birds sing. The bees buzz. The frogs call.

His garden.

He loves his garden.

He is ready.

He walks to his flat stone.

He closes his eyes.

He sleeps.
