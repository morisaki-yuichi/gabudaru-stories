March 19

Gabu Sits Still

🐢 Gabu

Today Gabu tries something different. He tries to sit completely still.

He sits on his flat stone. He does not move his feet. He does not move his head. He does not move at all.

He waits.

A bee flies past. He does not look at it.

A bird lands near the pond. He does not move to see it.

A leaf falls from the apple tree. He hears it land. He does not look.

He is still.

He breathes very slowly. In and out. Very slow.

Minutes go by.

Then something happens.

A butterfly lands on his head.

He feels the tiny weight of it. Very light. Like nothing. But something.

He does not move.

The butterfly stays there for a moment. Then it flies away.

Then a bird comes and sits on the ground near him. Very close. It looks at him. It hops a little closer.

He does not move.

The bird looks at him for a long time. Then it walks away slowly.

He thinks: when I am still, the world comes to me.

He thinks about this. He is usually always moving. Slowly, but moving. Walking from place to place. Looking at things.

But when he is still, things come to him.

He stays still until he is a little stiff.

Then he moves. Slowly. He stretches.

He walks to the pond.

He thinks: I learned something today.

He closes his eyes.

He is warm.

He sleeps.
