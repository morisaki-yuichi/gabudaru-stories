January 27

Gabu Is Happy

🐢 Gabu

Today Gabu wakes up feeling happy. He does not know why. The sky is grey. It is cold. Nothing is different from yesterday.

But he is happy.

He walks to his flat stone and stands on it. He stretches each leg. He looks around.

He thinks: I am happy today.

He walks to the yellow flower. He looks at it. It is still there. He is happy about that.

He walks to the big tree. He looks up. A bird is there. He is happy about that too.

He walks to the pond. The water is still. He sees his reflection. He smiles at his reflection.

He thinks: is the reflection smiling too? Yes. Yes it is.

He is happy that his reflection is also happy.

He walks around the whole garden. He goes slowly. He looks at every stone in the wall. He looks at the gate. He looks at the path. He looks at the leaf pile. He looks at the corner where the fern grows.

Everything is the same as yesterday. But today everything looks a little better.

"I live in a good garden," says Gabu.

He sits down in the middle of the garden. He looks up at the sky. The grey clouds are moving. Behind them, sometimes, a little blue.

He thinks: there is blue sky above the clouds. Even when I cannot see it.

He is happy.

He stays in the middle of the garden for a long time. He does not go anywhere else.

When evening comes, he walks back to his flat stone.

He closes his eyes.

He is still happy.

He does not know why. But he does not need to know.

He sleeps, happy.
