February 8

Gabu Is Lonely

🐢 Gabu

Today Gabu is lonely.

He does not know why it is today and not yesterday. He was happy yesterday. But today, he walks around his garden and he feels empty.

He walks to the pond. He looks at his reflection. Just him. One turtle.

He walks to the big tree. He looks up. No bird today. Just branches and sky.

He walks to the gate. He looks through the crack. Just the path. Empty.

He walks to his flat stone. He sits.

He thinks: it would be nice to have a friend. Someone to walk to the pond with. Someone to look at the sky with. Someone to talk to who would talk back.

The birds are nice. The snail is nice. But they do not stay. They come and go.

He looks at his three stones. The big flat one. The small grey one. The blue one.

"You are my friends," he says.

He knows stones are not really friends. Stones do not talk. Stones do not walk with you. But they stay. They are always here.

He thinks about this for a while.

He says: "It is all right to be lonely sometimes."

He is not angry about it. He is just honest.

He watches the afternoon go by. A bird comes to the tree. He says hello. The bird sings a little and flies away.

He thinks: I am here. My garden is here. Good things come sometimes. I will wait.

When evening comes, the sky turns pink and orange. He looks at it for a long time.

It is beautiful, even when you are lonely.

He goes to his flat stone.

He closes his eyes.

He sleeps.
