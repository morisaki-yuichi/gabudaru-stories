February 26

Something Is Coming

🐢 Gabu

Gabu feels it again today. The feeling he has had before. The feeling of something about to happen.

He walks around the garden slowly. He pays attention to everything.

The buds on the tree are now clearly visible from far away. He does not have to go close to see them.

The flowers on the south wall are many. More every day.

The frog is awake. He sees it often now.

The birds in the tree come every morning. Two or three at a time.

Everything is moving. Waking. Getting ready.

He goes to the gate. He listens. He hears sounds from outside. The wind in grass. Birds. And something else. Soft footsteps? He is not sure.

He presses his ear to the gate. He listens very carefully.

Yes. Someone on the path outside. Footsteps. Walking slowly.

He waits.

The footsteps get closer. Then they stop. Right outside the gate.

He is very still.

Then he hears it. That high, thin sound he heard in January. Once. Clear.

He does not know what it is. He does not know who is making it.

But he feels: this is someone good. He does not know how he knows. He just knows.

He puts his foot on the gate. He does not push. He just touches it.

The footsteps start again. And they walk away.

He listens until he cannot hear them anymore.

He walks back to his flat stone.

He sits.

He thinks: something is coming. I do not know when. I do not know what.

But it feels good.

He closes his eyes.

He sleeps.
